About

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA, advised by Prof. Dejan Marković. My research focuses on domain-specific hardware architectures, runtime reconfigurable arrays, and low-latency on-chip scheduling for ML and DSP workloads.

Research Interests

  • Runtime Reconfigurable Architectures (CGRAs)
  • On-chip Scheduling and Resource Management
  • ML and DSP Accelerators
  • ASIC Design and VLSI Systems
  • Energy- and Area-Efficient Hardware

Publications
